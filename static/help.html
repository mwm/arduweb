Quick help for Bulls and Cows

This is a trivial SMS-based implementation of the classic game Bulls and Cows (or Cows and Bulls or MasterMind).

The computer has chosen a string of four digits. The digits are drawn from '0' to '9', inclusive. Each digit can only occur once in the computers guess. Your job is to guess what the four digits are.

On your part, a move consists of texting a guess - a sequence of 4 unique digits - to (405) 283-6344. If the move doesn't have 4 unique digits, it's invalid. When the guess matches the chosen string, you win.

In return, you'll get back a text telling you that the move was invalid (if it was), or that you won and in how many moves, and the move number along with how many bulls and cows you have. A bull represents the correct digit in the correct place in the number, a cow a correct digit but not in the correct place.

Simple, right? Any game can be solved in seven turns. Good luck.

At this point, this is very much a test implementation. Please be gentle with it, and report problems to me.
